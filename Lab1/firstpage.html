Welcome to VIT-AP University

VIT-AP University, located in Amaravati, Andhra Pradesh, is one of the fastest-growing institutions for higher education in India. It is part of the VIT group of institutions, known for quality education, strong industry connect, and excellent placement records.

The university offers cutting-edge programs in Engineering, Sciences, Business, and Liberal Arts, designed to prepare students for global careers and research opportunities.

Campus Life

VIT-AP offers a vibrant campus life with student clubs, technical chapters, cultural fests, sports events, and hackathons. Students get ample opportunities to build leadership, teamwork, and communication skills.

Academic Excellence

The curriculum is designed with a Choice Based Credit System (CBCS) and Fully Flexible Credit System (FFCS), allowing students to choose courses, faculty, and timings to suit their academic goals.

Industry Connect

With regular guest lectures, workshops, internships, and industry projects, students get hands-on exposure and insights into real-world applications of what they learn in the classroom.
